The majority of companies moved their facilities to Lviv (24.6% of relocated enterprises), Zakarpattia (16.1%) and Chernivtsi (11.4%) regions. This is reported by the Ministry of Economy. And as of July 15, 678 enterprises were relocated to safe regions under the relocation program. Of them, 68% (467 enterprises) have already resumed their work. Among the enterprises that have already resumed their activities at the new location, the largest share is made up of enterprises of wholesale and retail trade, repair of motor vehicles and motorcycles (38.7%), processing industry (33.1%), information and telecommunications (5.9%) , professional, scientific and technical activities (5.3%). During the week (July 8-15), some gas station chains reduced prices for diesel fuel by an average of 30 kopecks/l to UAH 56.19/l.
